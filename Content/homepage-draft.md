## What I Do

Scope and pricing get built around your actual problem.

### Technical SEO

Rendering fixes, crawl budget, schema, and Core Web Vitals. Also, the technical groundwork most content teams never touch. I code too. Therefore, I work directly with JavaScript, React, and Next.js setups. So I don't hand dev teams vague tickets. Instead, I open pull requests. Furthermore, this matters most on JS-heavy sites where SSR is the difference between ranking and invisibility.

### Semantic and On-Page

On-page work driven by real SERP data, not keyword tool guesses. First, every page rewrite starts with a TF-IDF and NLP entity analysis of the top 10 ranking URLs. Therefore, you get a template that matches what Google actually rewards. Then it pushes past on underserved entities. Also covers internal linking, content pruning, and topical clustering.

### Link Building

Digital PR, sponsored data studies, and brand mention reclamation. Each campaign is built around your backlink profile shape. For example, a wide natural anchor distribution earns a more aggressive commercial push. However, a thinner profile gets slower, safer pacing.

### AI Agent Workflows for SEO

Custom n8n and Claude Code workflows that scale the work a solo consultant could never do by hand. Two core use cases stand out. First, SERP and TF-IDF analysis across hundreds of queries at once. Second, semantic and E-E-A-T audits that flag issues across entire sites in hours instead of weeks. Also, content briefs and quality checks are available as add-ons.
